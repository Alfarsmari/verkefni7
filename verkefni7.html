Erykah Badu, an iconic figure in the music and fashion world, is celebrated for her eclectic and bold fashion style that effortlessly blends vintage, bohemian, and Afrocentric influences. Her fashion choices reflect her artistic and spiritual journey, often featuring African patterns, vibrant colors, and a mix of textures that complement her neo-soul vibe. Erykah Badu's style is not just about clothing; it's an extension of her music and philosophy, embodying creativity, individuality, and a deep connection to her roots. Through her fashion, Badu has become a style icon, inspiring others to embrace their uniqueness and express themselves boldly and authentically. Erykah Badu has masterfully used her platform to celebrate her African roots, often donning garments and accessories that pay homage to African queens and warriors. The lyrics of "Certainly" reflect a strong, confident woman asserting her autonomy and self-respect in the face of a relationship that challenges her integrity. Badu's delivery is both smooth and powerful, conveying a sense of self-assurance and independence that resonates with listeners. The musical arrangement of "Certainly" complements its lyrical depth, featuring a laid-back groove that allows Badu's vocal performance to shine. The song's instrumentation, including its subtle use of drums, bass, and keyboards, creates a rich, organic sound that supports the song's message of empowerment and self-reliance.

"Certainly" not only demonstrates Badu's vocal talent and stylistic versatility but also her skillful songwriting.
